Welcome to the page of our project APM.

APM, which is short for Apm Power Manager, is a battery management application for Android phone. We build this app to provide an easy use, elegant yet powerful battery management for your Android phone.

Features

  • Current battery status, including the quantity, voltage, total capacity and temperature of the battery in your phone.
  • A precise estimation of your phone's working time under different scenario.
  • A comprehensive analysis of each application's hardware resources consumption, to help you locate, kill, or uninstall ill-behaviored applications and malicious software which occupy CPU and network resources, protecting your phone from dead battery and mobile dataplan exceeding.
  • Elegant UI following Material Design.
